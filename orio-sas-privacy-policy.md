## 12. Subscription and Consumable Items

### 12.1. Free Trial
Orio SAS offers a **14-day free trial period** during which users have full access to all app features. After the trial period expires, a valid subscription is required to continue using the app.

### 12.2. Subscriptions
- **Renewal:** Subscriptions are billed on a recurring basis and automatically renew at the end of each billing cycle unless canceled by the user prior to renewal.
- **Included Resellers:** With each active subscription, users receive **2 default resellers**, which renew automatically alongside the subscription.

### 12.3. Consumables - Additional Resellers
- **Purchase:** Users can purchase additional resellers as needed through in-app purchases.
- **Renewal:** Additional resellers are **consumable and renewable**. When a subscription ends, any additional resellers purchased will **not automatically renew** and must be repurchased upon subscription renewal.
  
### 12.4. Payment Processing
- **Payment Method:** All payments for subscriptions and additional resellers are processed securely through **ZainCash**, a local payment service in Iraq.
- **Payment Information Collected:** To process payments, we collect necessary information such as your **ZainCash account details**, transaction ID, and payment amount.
- **Security Measures:** We employ industry-standard encryption and security protocols to protect your payment information during transmission and storage. We do not store full payment details on our servers; sensitive payment data is handled directly by ZainCash in accordance with their privacy and security policies.
  
### 12.5. Refund Policy
- **24-Hour Refund Window:** We offer a **24-hour refund policy** for all subscription and additional reseller purchases. If you are not satisfied with your purchase or have made it in error, you may request a refund within 24 hours of the transaction.
- **Refund Process:**
  - To request a refund, please contact our support team at **oriomonitor@gmail.com** with your account details and transaction information.
  - Refunds will be processed through ZainCash and may take up to **5 business days** to reflect in your account, depending on ZainCash's processing times.
- **Non-Refundable Cases:** Refund requests made after the 24-hour window or for services already rendered may not be eligible for a refund, except as required by applicable law.
  
### 12.6. Cancellation Policy
- **Subscription Cancellation:** You may cancel your subscription at any time through the app settings or by contacting our support team. Cancellation will take effect at the end of the current billing cycle, and you will retain access to premium features until then.
- **Effect of Cancellation:** Upon cancellation, access to premium features and any additional resellers will expire at the end of the billing cycle. To regain access, a new subscription must be purchased.
